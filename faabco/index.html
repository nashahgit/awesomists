Coco Pith

Coco Pith is the residue after the Extraction of Fiber from the coconut husk It is a spongy material that binds the coconut fiber in the husk. It is an Organic matter and completely biodegradable and ecofriendly. It is free from any admixtured heavy metals. It is a mixture of corky cellular material and short fibers and has both the appearance and feel of peat moss. Coir pith is finding new applications in this new era of micro farming. With its moisture retention qualities, coir pith is an excellent multipurpose growing medium It act as a soil conditioner and is being extensively used as a soil-less medium for Agri-Horticultural purposes. It also contains high quality of nutrients that keep the soil healthy in a natural way. It is also used as combustible fuel in boilers in some countries as the colorific of coir pith is high. Its water retaining capacity, compressibility and light weight are the main characteristics which make it widely popular. Besides, it has acceptable pH, EC. and CEC and has excellent wettability superior to peat.

Salient applications

1. As a substrate for floriculture and horticulture crops.
2. As a potting mix.
3. As a soil conditioner/improver.
4. As a rooting media.
5. For golf courses and nursery poly bags.
6. As a mulch.

Coir Fibers

Coir is a natural Fiber extracted from the husk of coconut. coir is the fibrous material found between the hard, internal shell and the outer coat of a coconut. The coir fiber is relatively waterproof, and is one of the few natural fibers resistant to damage by saltwater. The thickest and most resistant of all commercial natural fibers. Its low decomposition rate means is a key advantage for making durable geo-textiles. The coir fiber has one of the highest concentrations of lignin, making it stronger and it has good resistance to microbial action hence needs no chemical treatment. Coir is widely used in the upholstery industry, and it is a healthy substitute for processed synthetic rubber. It is also used as a combination with natural rubber and is used for filling up mattresses, automobile seats, sofas, settees, and seating systems. Coir is used for insulation and finds application in panels, cold storages, food industry, etc.

Salient applications

• Bedding : Coir Fiber can be used for the manufacturing of mattress.
• Cushion Seating : it is used to Cushion Car Seats,Sofas and Furniture.
• Filling :Used as filling material for Coir Logs.
• Erosion Control : Coir Fiber can be used to produce Stitch Blankets-which are used to control Soil Erosion.
• Filtering Material :Used in Water Filtering.
• Acoustic and Thermal Barriers :Used to prevent Heat Transfer and for Sound-Proofing.

Coconut Shell Powder

The shell powder is manufactured from the coconut shell. The powder has various uses as a filler in synthetic resin glues, filler and extender in phenolic molding powders, mosquito repellent coils, mastic adhesives, resin casting, bituminous products etc. Since it has good durability characteristics, high toughness and abrasion resistant properties, it is suitable for long standing use. In the Granular form, it is used as a Lost Circulation Material in Oil Well Drilling . Coconut Shell Powder is preferred to other alternate materials available because of its uniformity in quality and chemical composition, better properties in respect of water absorption and resistance to fungal attack.
